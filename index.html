    Orthopedic and sports

    Helping you Move Toward Recovery

    Orthopedic and sports physiotherapy can help individuals across all ages, conditions, and fitness levels. Whether you have experienced a sports injury or your discomfort arose gradually over time, every treatment plan begins with a thorough assessment so that our physiotherapists can determine a proper diagnosis and treatment plan.     Pelvic Floor Therapy

    Your Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy

    Every physiotherapy journey begins with a careful evaluation of your medical history and current condition. Our pelvic pain specialists will gauge posture, strength, and flexibility to determine the extent of your pelvic condition. With this information, we can begin to build an effective plan based on exercise, lifestyle change, manual therapy, and electrotherapy.
